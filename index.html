About

I started programming first semester of my Freshman year, and I've been addicted ever since. The past 3 years I've learned about programming through classes and through my own exploration and side projects. But the most important thing I've learned is that I can use this medium to express myself. I can create something and share it with others, even if they have no clue how to program. And it all starts with an empty folder, or as I like to think of it, a blank canvas.

Other Interests

Alien Beef

The simple mobile game where you abduct "beef creatures" from un-happy farmers. They respond by firing pitchforks into the air as a last ditch attempt to retrieve their stolen cattle.

Powered by: Cocos2D.
Artwork: Everything (except for the cow) is original.
Music: Jake Sullivan.


  • Website: http://alienbeef.com
